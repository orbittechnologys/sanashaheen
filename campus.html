Sana Shaheen PU College organizes orientation programs on various subjects are offered to students and faculty. Recreation trip is organized at the end of every academic year.

LABORATORIES Physics, Chemistry, Home Science, Biology and Electronics laboratories are well equipped with the Latest scientific apparatus, charts, specimens, models and instrumentations for conducting practical course work and projects.
